Overview

At MentorHub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our mentorship platform.

By using MentorHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password, profile picture
  • Profile Data: Bio, skills, experience, education, location, timezone
  • Verification Documents: Identity documents, educational certificates, professional credentials
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party providers)

Session Data

  • Session Records: Session dates, duration, topics discussed, ratings and reviews
  • Communication: Messages exchanged through our platform, session recordings (with consent)
  • Meeting Information: Google Meet links, calendar integrations, session materials

Technical Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Analytics: Performance metrics, error logs, user interaction patterns

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision
  • • Create and manage your account
  • • Match students with mentors
  • • Facilitate sessions and communications
  • • Process payments and transactions
Safety & Verification
  • • Verify mentor credentials
  • • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • • Ensure platform safety
  • • Compliance with legal requirements
Platform Improvement
  • • Analyze usage patterns
  • • Improve our services
  • • Develop new features
  • • Optimize user experience
Communication
  • • Send service notifications
  • • Provide customer support
  • • Share platform updates
  • • Marketing communications (with consent)

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Profile information, ratings, and reviews are visible to help users make informed decisions
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our platform (payment processors, cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or sale of assets (with user notification)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption
Data encrypted in transit and at rest
Secure Storage
Industry-standard secure servers
Access Controls
Limited access to authorized personnel

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and update our security practices to maintain the highest level of protection.
