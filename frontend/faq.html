General Questions

MentorHub is a peer tutoring platform that connects students with verified mentors across various subjects and skills. Our platform facilitates personalized learning experiences through one-on-one sessions, group tutorials, and comprehensive mentorship programs.

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, complete your profile, and upload required verification documents. Once approved, you can browse mentors, book sessions, and begin your learning journey. Students can start immediately, while mentors go through a verification process.

Creating an account and browsing mentors is completely free. You only pay for the mentoring sessions you book. Each mentor sets their own hourly rate, and you can filter by price range to find options that fit your budget.

Finding Mentors

Use our advanced search filters to find mentors by subject expertise, availability, hourly rate, location, and student ratings. You can also read mentor profiles, view their qualifications, and check reviews from other students before booking a session.

Yes! All mentors go through a comprehensive verification process. We check their credentials, educational background, and professional experience. Mentors must provide valid documentation and pass our quality standards before they can offer sessions on the platform.

Sessions & Scheduling

To book a session, find your desired mentor, click "Book Session," select your preferred date and time, choose session duration, and provide any specific topics you'd like to cover. The mentor will receive your request and can accept or suggest alternative times.

You can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session may incur a cancellation fee. Emergency cancellations are handled case-by-case.

Sessions are conducted via video call using integrated Google Meet links. You'll receive the meeting link before your session starts. Sessions include screen sharing, document collaboration, and recording options (with consent). All sessions are private and secure.

Payments & Billing

Payments are processed securely when you book a session. We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is held in escrow and released to the mentor after the session is completed. You'll receive a detailed receipt for each transaction.

We offer full refunds for sessions cancelled 24+ hours in advance. If you're unsatisfied with a session, contact our support team within 24 hours for a review. We may offer partial or full refunds based on the circumstances and mentor feedback.

MentorHub charges a small service fee (typically 5-10%) added to the mentor's hourly rate. This fee covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and customer support. The total cost is clearly displayed before you complete your booking.

Technical Support

MentorHub works on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). For video sessions, you'll need a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone. We recommend using a desktop or laptop for the best experience, though mobile devices are supported.

Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you're still having issues, clear your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. Contact our technical support team if problems persist.

Contact our support team through the help center or email technical@mentorhub.com. Include details about your browser, operating system, and steps to reproduce the issue. Our technical team typically responds within 2-4 hours during business hours.
